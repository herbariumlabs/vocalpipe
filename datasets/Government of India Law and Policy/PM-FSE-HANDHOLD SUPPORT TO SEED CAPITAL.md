  INSTRUCTION FOR HANDHOLD SUPPORT TO SEED CAPITAL BENEFICIARIES BY STATE RURAL LIVELIHOOD MISSION (SRLM)/ STATE URBAN LIVELIHOOD MISSION (SULM)

1.  INTRODUCTION:

  Under the PM Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises Scheme (PMFME) Scheme, Seed Capital @ Rs. 40,000/- per member of SHG with a maximum ceiling of 4 lakh per SHG for working capital and purchase of small tools would be given as grant to the SHG federation by State Nodal Agency (SNA)/ State Rural Livelihood Mission (SRLM)/ State Urban Livelihood Mission (SULM) for SHGs under food processing activity.

  MoFPI and MoRD/ MoHUA have been working together to ensure that SHG members are able to avail the benefits of the scheme, especially in the component to support SHGs. The State Rural Livelihood Missions (SRLMs) and State Urban livelihood Mission (SULM) under DAY- NRLM & DAY-NULM and the designated State Nodal Agency (SNA) appointed by the State Governments for this scheme are working closely for providing scheme benefits to the target beneficiaries.

2.  Role of SRLM/SULM:

    a.  SRLM/SULM are responsible for the identification and collection of data regarding SHG members involved in food processing activities and interested to enhance their business through capital investment and working capital. SHGs are federated at Community based organizations (CBOs)- Village Organization (VOs)/ City Level Federation (CLF)/ Area Level Federation (ALF)/Cluster Level Federation.

    b.  Based on the recommendation from Mission Director, SRLMs/ SULMs, applications are approved by Principal Secretary/Secretary of the SNA Department. Seed Capital is released to SRLM/SULM for further transferring to CLF/VO/ALF which is given as a loan to the SHG account in case of individual application or food processing group account in case of group application.

  For the above responsibilities indicated, a Joint Letter has been signed between MoFPI & MoRD on 20^(th) September, 2020 and MoFPI & MoHUA on 9^(th) June, 2021. Joint seed capital guidelines issued with MoRD on 26^(th) November, 2020 and with MoHUA on 18th June, 2021 (attached at Annexure).

3.  Role of SNA:

i.  Timely approval and release of Seed Capital applications to SRLM/SULM with approval from Competent Authority of SNA.

ii. SNA would monitor the process of transfer of Seed Capital from SRLM/SULM to SHG members and further utilization of Seed Capital by SHG enterprises.

iii. SNA would conduct regular meetings with SRLM/SULM at least once in a month. SNA should seek report of transfer status and utilization report from SRLM/SULM for each batch of Seed Capital amount released.

     1.  Handhold Cost:

  Handhold cost of Rs. 2,437 per enterprise is approved for implementing Seed Capital component for:

- Identification and mobilization of SHG beneficiaries,

- Collection of information for an application form from the applicant,

- Facilitation of recommendations from SHG, Village Organization (VO), Cluster Level Federation (CLF), Area Level Federation (ALF)

- MIS entry in the NRLM/NULM MIS in the application form

- Scrutiny of Seed Capital applications

- Revisiting enterprises for rejected applications and updating information

- Tracking of enterprises for fund utilization

- Update information on fund utilization & repayment in NRLM/NULM MIS

- Facilitation of training and formalization of SHG units

  The handhold cost of Rs. 2,437 per enterprise would be transferred to SRLM/SULM upon approval from SNA.

1.  Procedure of Release of Funds-

  The fund for Handholding cost is a non-sharable component and 100% would be borne by Ministry. The fund will be transferred from Ministry in the SNA account. SNA would release the fund in the account of SRLM/SULM based on the recommendation from SRLM/SULM.

  The handhold cost of Rs. 2,437/- per enterprise application would be released in two tranches, Rs. 1000/- for the mobilization and filing of application for SHG enterprises seeking Seed Capital and the next tranche of Rs. 1,437/- would be provided after the facilitation for training of Seed Capital beneficiaries, facilitation for FSSAI registration and Udyam Aadhaar registration as per the applicability for the unit.

  Steps for the release of Funds-

i.  SRLM/SULM would claim the handhold cost against the recommendation of Seed Capital applications submitted to SNA. Handhold cost should be calculated and claimed against the number of Seed capital applications for the SHG enterprises recommended. Claim could be done at the time of recommendation of Seed capital applications with the certification from Mission Director on the data authenticity and Seed Capital amount.

ii. The SNA in the State would sanction the claimed amount by SRLM/SULM for Seed Capital Disbursement and handhold cost claimed, which should be subsequently ratified in SLAC.

iii. The details of recommended list should be available in the Seed Capital MIS of NRLM/NULM. SRLM/SULM would share the complete list of information as per the Seed Capital guidelines to SNA through excel sheet and in the format that can be uploaded in the PMFME MIS as per the requirement.

iv. The first tranche of handhold cost of Rs. 1,000/- would be released by SNA to SRLM/SULM against the approved number of Seed Capital applications. SNA would transfer the consolidated amount of Seed Capital and first tranche of handhold cost against the approved proposals to the designated account of SRLM/SULM.

v.  SRLM/SULM would provide the utilization report to SNA for transfer of funds to VO/CLF/ALF and then to SHG members as updated in the NRLM/NULM MIS and would share the report in the format given at Annexure I & II.

vi. The second tranche of Rs. 1,437/- handhold cost against the approved Seed Capital applications would be released post completion of the activities as in facilitation for training of Seed Capital beneficiaries (SHG member), FSSAI registration and Udyam Aadhaar registration as per the applicability of the food processing unit along with submission of utilization report of Seed Capital amount. The amount for Individual/Group SHG unit would be collected and updated in the NRLM/NULM MIS as per Annexure III on –

    - Transfer of Seed Capital amount

    - Equipment purchased as proposed

    - Repayment of Seed Capital amount to SHG Federation

    - Data Validation

    - Two-time tracking of SHG unit.

- Till the time of development of NRLM/NULM MIS-Utilization module, utilization report should be provided in the hardcopy with the signature of Mission Director.

- SRLM/ SULM would share the utilization report of Seed Capital disbursement with the certification from Mission Director, SRLM/ SULM on fortnightly basis with the SNA:

  - Funds are transferred from SRLM/ SULM to CLF/ VO/ALF providing details with the specified field as indicated in the Annexure I.

  - Fund are transferred from CLF/ VO to SHG /Group account as indicated in the Annexure II.

  - Utilization Report as per the Annexure III

vii. For the cases where applicant is not interested for Seed Capital after the approval of the application and release of Seed Capital fund from SNA, Seed Capital amount and handhold cost released should be returned to SNA account.
