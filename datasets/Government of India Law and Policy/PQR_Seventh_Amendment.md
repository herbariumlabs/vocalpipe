  S.O. (E). - In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Destructive Insects and Pests Act, 1914 (2 of 1914), the Central Government hereby makes the following Order further to amend the Plant Quarantine (Regulation of Import into India) Order, 2003, namely: -

1.  Short title and Commencement. - (1) This Order may be called the Plant

  Quarantine (Regulation of Import into India) (Seventh Amendment) Order, 2023.

  (2) It shall come into force on the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.
