SHORT TITLE, EXTENT AND COMMENCEMENT:

  (1) This Order may be called the Assam Paddy and Rice Procurement (Levy and Licensing) (Amendment) Permanent Order, 2000.

(2) It extends to the whole of the Stare of Assam.

(3) It shall come into force on the date of its publication in the official Gazette.
