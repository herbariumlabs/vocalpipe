  OFFICE MEMORANDUM

Subject: Procedure for release of funds from SNA to Host Institutes for establishment of Common Incubation Center.

  With reference to the Department of Expenditure, PFMS Division O.M No. F. No. I( 13)PFMS/FCD/2020 dated 23.03.2021 regarding "Procedure for release of funds under Centrally Sponsored Schemes (CSS) and monitoring utilization of the funds released'', all the State/UTs were requested to strictly follow the procedure for implementation of the PMFME Scheme.

2.  On the issue of opening of separate saving bank account by the SNA & zero balance subsidiary account by the Implementing Agencies, some SNAs/Host Institutes informed about procedural problems being faced by them.

3.  The matter has been reviewed & with the approval of the Competent Authority it is informed that the funds for establishment of Incubation Center shall be released to SNA. SNA shall release the funds to the Saving Bank account of Host Institutes i.e. there is no requirement of separate account for SNA & zero balance subsidiary account for Host Institutes.

4.  All States/ UTs are requested to take necessary action accordingly & funds be released to Host Institutes at the earliest so that the establishment of Incubation Center is not delayed.
