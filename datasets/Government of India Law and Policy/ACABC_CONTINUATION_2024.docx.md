6. The budgetary provision under the ACABC Component during the FY 2024-25 for Training and Subsidy Component will be Rs. 2000.00 lakhs and Rs. 1500.00 lakhs with these components being implemented by MANAGE, Hyderabad and NABARD, Mumbai respectively. The funds under ACABC Component will be released in the form of Grants-in Aid to the Implementing Agency.

7. The Implementing Agencies in respect of all Components of the Central Sector Plan Scheme will be required to furnish Audited Utilization Certificates in the prescribed proforma (GFR 19A) to this Department immediately for the grants released during 2022-23. Provisional UCs may be required to be furnished in respect of funds released during 2023-24 clearly indicating the unspent balance as on 01.04.2024 to enable revalidation of the same for utilization during FY 2024\-25, if required.

8. **Support to State Extension Programme for Extension Reforms (ATMA Component)**

   1) The pattern of funding for the Scheme shall be in the ratio of 60:40 (Centre: State) for General States and 90:10(Centre/ State) for North-Eastern States & two Himalayan States (Himachal Pradesh and Uttarakhand) for all components except Farmer Friend (FF}, for which, it will be on 50:50 basis. However, funding for UTs (without legislature) will be 100% from GOI for all components.

   2) Funds will be released as per demands projected by States/UTs through their State Extension Work Plan (SEWP) after it has been approved by State Level Sanctioning Committee (SLSC) of respective State/ Executive Committee in the DA\&FW.

   3) Monthly Progress Report on physical and financial achievements will be furnished by States/UTs implementing agencies etc. on Extension Reforms Monitoring System (ERMS) portal ([http://extensionreforms.dacnet.nic/in/)](http://extensionreforms.dacnet.nic/in/\)) regularly and no physical progress report would be entertained

w.e.f. 01.04.2024, to concerned Scheme Officers of Directorate of Extension, New Delhi. Directorate of Extension will compile these monthly progress reports and submit the same to Department of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare in consolidated form with their comments.
