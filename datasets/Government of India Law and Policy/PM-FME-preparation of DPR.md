OFFICE MEMORANDUM

  Subject: Clarification regarding assistance of Rs. 50,000/- per case to FPOs/ SHGs/ Cooperatives for preparation of DPR under the PMFME Scheme, MoFPI.

  As part of Aatmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan, the Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) has launched an all India centrally sponsored “ Prime Minister Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME) scheme” for providing financial, technical, and business support for the micro food processing enterprises. Support will also be provided to Farmer Producer Organization (FPOs), Self Help Groups (SHGs) and Producers Cooperatives.

2.  In accordance to the scheme guidelines, “Assistance of Rs. 50,000/- per case would be provided to FPOs/ SHGs/ Cooperatives for preparation of DPR”.

3.  It is clarified that the applications with DPR submitted from FPOs/ SHGs/ Cooperatives to SNA/online MIS portal are eligible for the assistance. Payment for DPR preparation will be made only in the bank account of the concerned FPOs/ SHGs/ Cooperatives via NEFT/RTGS.

4.  Assistance fee will be as per actual or Rs. 50,000, whichever is less. Applicant has to claim the assistance fee by submitting a proper invoice. States/UTs should ensure that the DPR is in accordance to the DPR structure issued vide OM dated 25^(th) January 2021(copy attached). and the payment is to be made in accordance to the Department of Expenditure O.M. dated 23^(rd) March 2021(Copy attached).

5.  This has the approval of the competent authority.
