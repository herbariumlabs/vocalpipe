  Subject : Notification to be made in the SPS Committee of WTO regarding protocols for trial shipments for import of grains and pulses with in-transit and pre­ shipment Phosphine fumigation -reg.

  The undersigned is directed to enclose herewith the text of the two protocols for trial shipment for import of grains and pulses with in-transit/pre shipment phosphine fumigation from MBr phased out countries.
