# **OFFICE MEMORANDUM**

**Sub: Broad parameters/ criteria for proposing any schemes for the financial support from National CAMPA as formulated by Executive Committee in its 30th Meeting- reg**

It is informed that the Executive Committee in its 30th meeting held on *Oih* June, 2024 under the Chairmanship of the Director General of Forests and Special Secretary (DGF\&SS), MoEF\&CC formulated following broad parameters/ criteria for formulating/ proposing any scheme for the financial support from National CAMPA: .

1\.	The CAMPA Funds are an additional funds and should not be seen as an alternative to the regular budgetary financial support.

2. The scheme should be new or a pilot to initiate or implement an innovative

   idea/ technologyto address any gap(s) in the present research, conservation programmes as noted in the para 5 (ii) above and should not be an arrangement for meeting the shortage of funds in an ongoing Schemes or Programmes.

3. The scheme should have clearly defined programme objectives and timelines with measurable and monitorable outcomes, sunset clause and exit strategy. The exit strategy should include proper handholding to stakeholder departments and organisations to continue/sustain the outcomes or the gains made under the scheme without depending upon the National CAMPA Fund for indefinite long period.  
4. The Scheme may be taken up as a model of convergence but its activities should not be overlapping with any other Central Sponsored Scheme, Central Sector Scheme & State's Programmes & Schemes.

5. The Programme Division should also inform about the efforts made to explore funding of the Scheme form their regular as well as other alternative sources.

6. Further, it has to be ensured by the Implementing Institution and the Programme Division that there will not be any liability on account of legal, establishment, commercial or of any other formon National CAMPA for financially supporting the implementation of the scheme or after its completion.

v11\.	While submitting the proposal for the scheme, the programme division should

clearly mention that the proposal has the approval of the competent authority.

8. The programme division and implementing agency should have in-built continuous monitoring mechanism in the scheme and also have provision for

   mid-term review.
