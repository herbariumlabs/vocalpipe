OFFICE MEMORANDUM

Subject: Notification to be made in the SPS committee of WTO regarding Plant Quarantine (Regulation of Import into India) (Fourth Amendment), Order, 2023 - reg.

  The undersigned is directed to enclose herewith the text of the draft notification (in WTO format) of the Plant Quarantine (Regulation of Import into India) (Fourth Amendment), Order, 2023 proposing certain amendments to the phytosanitary conditions governing the import of fresh Avocado fruits (Persea americana) from Kenya into India.

2.  It is requested that further necessary action to notify the same to the WTO Secretariat may be taken by Department of Commerce (TPD Division) at the earliest and the WTO notification number allotted by the WTO Secretariat may please be intimated to this department. The text of the notification containing the Plant Quarantine (Regulation of import into India) (Fourth Amendment), Order, 2023 will be available at www.agricoop.nic.in under the head "Circulars and Notifications"­ Plant Protection-WTO Notifications.

3.  This issues with the approval of Joint Secretary (Plant Protection), DA&FW.
