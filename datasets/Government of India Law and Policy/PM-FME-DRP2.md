  OFFICE MEMORANDUM

  Subject: Amendments in the Guidelines for engagement of District Resource Persons under PM FME Scheme

  The undersigned is directed to refer to the para no. 9.3.8.2 of Guidelines for Implementation of PM FME Scheme issued on 19.6.2020 and to say that the qualification and experience for engagement of Resource Persons specified therein may be amended with the priority as under:

(i) First preference for District Resource Persons should be persons with Diploma/ Degree in food technology with experience.

(ii) Second preference for persons with diploma/ degree in food technology without experience

(iii) Third preference for persons with degree in agriculture and experience in food technology and preparing DPRs etc.

  This is for information and necessary action.

  This has the approval of the component authority.
