GOVERNMENT OF INDIA

  MINISTRY OF AGRICULTURE & FARMERS WELFARE DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE & FARMERS WELFARE

LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 417

  TO BE ANSWERED ON 5^(th) DECEMBER, 2023

PM-KISAN MAAN DHAN YOJANA

  417. SHRI RAM MOHAN NAIDU KINJARAPU:

Will the Minister of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare कृषि एवं ककसान कल्याण मंत्री be pleased to state:

(a) the number of eligible farmers in Andhra Pradesh who have been enrolled under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maan Dhan Yojana, year-wise;

(b) the number of farmers who have received money under the said Yojana, year-wise;

(c) the amount disbursed under the abovesaid Yojana to the enrolled farmers in Andhra Pradesh, year-wise;

(d) whether the Government has evaluated or assessed the impact and effectiveness of the aforesaid Yojana specifically in Andhra Pradesh; and

(e) if so, the details of the findings along with any subsequent actions taken so far?

ANSWER

  MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE

कृषि एवं ककसान कल्याण मंत्री (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)

(a) to (c) The Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maandhan Yojana, a central sector scheme, is a voluntary and contributory pension scheme for the Small and Marginal landholder farmers of entry age group of 18 to 40 years with a provision of Rs. 3000/- monthly pension on attaining the age of 60 years. As on date, total 23,38,309 farmers have been enrolled across the country and in Andhra Pradesh total 37,429 farmers have been enrolled under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maandhan Yojana since inception of the scheme.

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maandhan Yojana scheme was launched in 2019, with a provision of monthly pension of Rs. 3000/- after attaining the age of 60. The pension would start to the beneficiaries after attaining the age 60.

(d) & (e) The Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maandhan Yojana was launched in 2019, so far no impact study has been carried out.
