  OFFICE MEMORANDUM

Subject : Notification to be made in the SPS Committee of WTO regarding Plant Quarantine (Regulation of import into India) (Second Amendment), Order, 2022.

  The undersigned is directed to enclose herewith the text of the draft notification (in WTO format) of the Plant Quarantine (Regulation of import into India) (Second Amendment), Order, 2022 proposing certain amendments to the phytosanitary conditions governing the import of certain plants and plant materials into India. The notification includes import of fresh fruits of Sweet Cherry (Prunus avium) from USA.

2.  It is requested that further necessary action to notify the same to the WTO Secretariat may be taken by Department of Commerce (TPD Division) at the earliest and WTO notification number allotted by WTO Secretariat may please be intimated to this Department. The text of the notification containing the Plant Quarantine (Regulation of import into India) (Second Amendment), Order, 2022 will be available at www.agricoop.nic.in under the Head "Circulars and Notifications' - Plant Protection - WTO Notifications.

3.  This issues with the approval of Hon'able AM, Ministry of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare.
