**SCHEMES UNDER CMSGUY**

**Procurement of Cows, Heifers and Calves from Military farms under CMSGUY**

**Introduction:**  
Assam is hiring small-scale milk producers who have the potential to meet the required increased demand in milk & milk products in growing urban centers. Organized milk production & marketing of milk in the state of Assam remains relatively insignificant despite effort in the past to develop & promote collective milk.

**OBJECTIVE:**  
The scheme aims to improve the livelihood of dairy farmers & thus enhancing the production of milk & milk products in financial year 2018-19 by providing fund from CMSGUY.

The beneficiaries are the dairy Cooperative Society (DCS). The selected military farms for cattle induction includes Bengdrebi, Birnaguri, Panagarh and Guwahati.
