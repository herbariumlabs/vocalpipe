**SCHEMES UNDER CMSGUY**

**Workshop cum Training at Cultural Centres under CMSGUY**

**Introduction:**

The scheme “Workshop cum Training at Cultural Centres” was approved by MMS-CMSGUY in view to promote & preserve the Cultural Diversity of Folk Dances & music of various communities of Assam. Under this scheme, workshops on the various art forms will be held specific music, dance, folk and tribal dying art of Assam. This scheme will be implemented by Cultural Renaissance Mission Society (CRMS) \-CMSGUY with the help of the Directorate of Cultural Affairs. 

**Objective:**   
The main aim of the scheme “Workshop cum Training at Cultural Centres” under CMSGUY is to conduct 3 workshops at each 35 Cultural Centres of Assam to enable young persons to acquire skills in common art forms practiced in the society. The various art forms like music and dance of different communities of Assam like Bodo, Mishing, Karbi, Tiwa, Sonowal-Kachari, Rabha-Hajong, Deuri, Kosch etc. are covered under this scheme.

**Outcome:**

The scheme aims to conduct 3 nos. of workshop yearly by the 35 nos. of Cultural Centers in Assam which will promote and create skill on culture among the youths of Assam.
