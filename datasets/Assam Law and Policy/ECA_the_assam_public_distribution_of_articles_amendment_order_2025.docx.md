# **ORDERS BY THE GOVERNOR OF ASSAM NOTIFICATION**

**Dated: Dispur, the 20th March, 2025**

**eFile No- 276303/473:** In exercise of the powers conferred by section 3 of the Essential Commodities Act, 1955 (Act 10 of 1955\) read with the Government of India, Ministry of Agriculture and Irrigation (Department of Food) Order No. GSR. 800, dated the 9th of June, 1978, the Governor of Assam is pleased to amend the Assam Public Distribution of Articles Order, 1982, hereinafter referred to as the principal Order, in the following manner, namely:-

Short title and commencement

Amendment of clause 3\.

Amendment of FORM 1

1. (1) This order may be called as the Assam Public Distribution of Articles (Amendment) Order, 2025\.

(2) It shall come into force on the date of publication in the Official Gazette.

2. In the principal Order, in order No. 3, in the table, in entry C, under column RENEWAL FEE, for the words and numerical, "Rs. 45.00"(Notified vide gazette notification no.84, dated 7th February, 2022\) the words and numerical "Rs. 75.00" shall be

substituted.

3. In the principal Order (Notified vide gazette notification no.84, dated 7th February, 2022), in FORM 1, in terms and conditions, for the existing Sl.No. 11, for the words

and numerical, "3 years" the words and numerical "5 years" shall be substituted.
