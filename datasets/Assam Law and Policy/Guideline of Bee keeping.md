**ANNUAL ACTION PLAN & GUIDELINE FOR IMPLEMENTATION OF INTEGRATED DEVELOPMENT OF SCIENCTIFIC BEE-KEEPING (IDSB), A SUB SCHEME UNDER RASHTRIYA KRISHI VIKAS YOJONA (RKVY), SANCTIONED DURING THE YEAR 2016-17 AND IMPLEMENTED DURING THE YEAR 2017-18 AFTER REVALIDATION.**

This pilot project for IDSB for enhancing productivity of crops by pollination support through beekeeping is aimed all round development of Agriculture which consists of three major components:-

1) **Technical training support to the beneficiaries on scientific beekeeping by well acquainted resource person at training center.**  
2) **Subsidy sale of bee colonies, bee hives with super and bee equipment including Honey Extractor**

3) **Establishment of Primary Processing Plant in FPC- (During 2nd Phase)**

**Decision of Project Steering Committee (PIC):-**

1. As per decision of Project Implementation Committee (PIC), this program will be implemented in two Districts only namely Kamrup and Nagaon where there is need of pollination support for the crops like oilseeds, pulses, and other Horticulture crop.

2. The project should be implemented in cluster basis, so the committee suggested implementing the project through FPC, so that the Produced Honey can be purchased by FPC and the farmers can able to get satisfactory price. Moreover, there is a provision for construction of primary processing unit for Honey at FPC level.  
3. To motivate farmers there is provision of incentives/assistance in terms of input like Bee Hives, Bee Colony and equipments including honey extractor which can be purchased by a beneficiary by paying 60% of the cost. A beneficiary will get 40% subsidy and the 4o % of cost limited to 50 colonies, 50 bee hives and ten set of equipment per beneficiary.  
4. Focus will be given to marginal, small farmers along with young entrepreneur and women cultivator.  
5. For selection of beneficiary the Board of Director will do a meeting at Farmers Interested Group (FIG) and the CEO of respective company will take approval from Agriculture Development Officer of respective circle and must be countersigned by District Agriculture Officer/District Horticulture Officer/Responsible officer for Honey Bee scheme empowered by District Agriculture Officer.

6. The District Agriculture Officer, Nagaon/ Kamrup are requested to take initiative as per allotment to implement the project in time. Moreover DAO can mobilize CEO of the FPC for successful implementation of the program when the local officer is engaged in NRC and other work by the Govt.  
7. One entrepreneur must purchase at least minimum one unit (5 no of bee box, bee colony and one equipment) and maximum 10 numbers of bee units.  
8. The beneficiaries should undergo 7 days training program on beekeeping before purchasing the bee unit.
