**SCHEMES UNDER CMSGUY**

**Guru Sishya Monikanchan under CMSGUY**

**Introduction:**

The scheme “Guru Sishya Monikanchan” under CMSGUY was approved by the MMS-CMSGUY in view to preserve, promote \&propagate new talents in the fields of music, dance, folk and tribal art forms through ancient Indian System of Guru Sishya Parampara. This scheme is at per “Guru Sishya Parampara” scheme implemented by Govt. of India under Zonal Cultural Centre in 2003-04. This scheme will be implemented by Cultural Renaissance Mission Society (CRMS) \-CMSGUY with the help of the Directorate of Cultural Affairs. 

**Objective:** 

The main aim of the scheme ““Guru Sishya Monikanchan” under CMSGUY is to preserve and propagate rare & vanishing art forms whether classical and folk/tribal under the able guidance of Gurus so that young talents be nurtured to acquire skills in their chosen field of art through some financial assistance. The various art forms covered under this scheme are:

1. Sattriya Dance.  
2. Sattriya Traditional Wooden Craft  
3. Dhol, Khol, Doba, Nagara and all other Drums of ethenic Tribes  
4. Xasipaat  
5. Mridanga  
6. Flute  
7. Folk Dance of different Ethnic Groups of Assam  
8. Manipuri Dance   
9. Mask Making  
10. DihaNaam  
11. TokariGeet  
12. KamrupiLokageet  
13. GoalporiaLokageet  
14. BiyaNaam  
15. NisukaniGeet  
16. Bongeet  
17. Kushan  
18. Different types of Oja-pali  
19. Bor-Dhulia  
20. Dhepa-dhol  
21. Jikir-Jari  
22. Cherra-Dhek  
23. Kalia  
24. Puppertry  
25. Deodhani
